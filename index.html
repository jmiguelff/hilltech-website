What We Do

End-to-End Wind Technology

We deliver integrated solutions across every layer of the wind energy stack.

Power Electronics

Custom converters, frequency drives, and grid-interface power modules engineered for the harsh environments of on- and offshore wind farms. Our hardware maximises energy capture across the full operational envelope.

  • Full-power converter design
  • LVRT & grid-code compliance
  • Thermal & EMC management
  • Retrofit and upgrade programmes

Condition Monitoring

Real-time SCADA integration, vibration analytics, and predictive diagnostics keep your turbines running at peak performance while cutting unplanned downtime and O&M costs.

  • SCADA & data acquisition
  • Vibration & acoustic analysis
  • Predictive maintenance alerts
  • Remote fault diagnostics

Software Platforms

Cloud-native fleet management dashboards, digital-twin simulations, and API integrations that give operators unprecedented visibility and control over their entire wind portfolio.

  • Fleet management dashboard
  • Digital-twin simulations
  • REST & MQTT API integration
  • AI-powered yield optimisation

Control Systems

From pitch and yaw control to park-level active power management, our real-time embedded controllers are certified for safety-critical wind applications and continuously updated over-the-air.

  • Pitch & yaw control
  • Park-level power management
  • OTA firmware management
  • IEC 61400 certification support

Grid Analytics

Power quality monitoring, harmonic analysis, and grid-stability studies that help operators maintain compliance, optimise curtailment, and negotiate better grid connection agreements.

  • Power quality measurement
  • Harmonic & flicker analysis
  • Grid stability studies
  • Curtailment optimisation

Cybersecurity

IEC 62443-aligned security frameworks, encrypted communications, and 24/7 SOC monitoring protect your operational technology from increasingly sophisticated cyber threats targeting energy infrastructure.

  • IEC 62443 compliance
  • OT network segmentation
  • Encrypted SCADA comms
  • 24/7 SOC monitoring
